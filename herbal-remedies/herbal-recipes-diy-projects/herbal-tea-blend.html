Herbal Tea Blend for Digestion

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon peppermint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger root, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon chamomile flowers

Instructions:

  1. Combine the herbs in a teapot.
  2. Pour boiling water over the herbs and steep for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea and serve.

Benefits:

  • Aids digestion and relieves bloating
  • Soothes stomach upset
  • Promotes relaxation
