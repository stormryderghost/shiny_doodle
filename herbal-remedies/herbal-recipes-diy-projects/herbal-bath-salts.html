DIY Herbal Bath Salts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Epsom salt
  • 1/2 cup sea salt
  • 1/4 cup dried lavender
  • 1/4 cup dried chamomile
  • 10 drops lavender essential oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the Epsom salt, sea salt, lavender, and chamomile.
  2. Stir until well mixed.
  3. Add the lavender essential oil and stir again.
  4. Store the mixture in an airtight container.
  5. To use, add a handful of the bath salts to a warm bath and soak for 15-20 minutes.

Benefits:

  • Relaxes muscles and reduces stress
  • Promotes better sleep
  • Soothes skin and reduces inflammation
