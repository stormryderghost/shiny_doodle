Herbal Infused Sugar Scrub

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons dried herbs of your choice (e.g., rose petals, lavender)

Instructions:

  1. Combine the sugar, olive oil, and herbs in a jar.
  2. Stir until well combined.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
  4. To use, apply a handful of the scrub to your skin and gently massage in circular motions.

Benefits:

  • Exfoliates and softens skin
  • Leaves skin feeling refreshed and hydrated
  • Provides a relaxing aromatherapy experience
