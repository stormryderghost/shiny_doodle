Herbal Face Mask

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon ground oatmeal
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine the oatmeal, honey, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. Apply the mask to your face and leave it on for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Rinse with warm water and pat dry.

Benefits:

  • Exfoliates and cleanses skin
  • Brightens complexion
  • Reduces inflammation
