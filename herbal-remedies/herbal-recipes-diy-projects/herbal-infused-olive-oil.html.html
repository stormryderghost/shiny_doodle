Herbal Infused Olive Oil

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup dried herbs of your choice (e.g., rosemary, thyme, lavender)

Instructions:

  1. Combine the olive oil and herbs in a jar.
  2. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-4 weeks.
  3. Strain the oil to remove the herbs.

Uses:

  • Drizzle over salads or roasted vegetables
  • Use as a massage oil
  • Add to bath salts for a fragrant soak
