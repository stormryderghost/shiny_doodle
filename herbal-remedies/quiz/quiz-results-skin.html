Herbal Remedies for Skin Health

For healthy, glowing skin, consider these herbs:

  • Calendula – Soothes irritated skin and promotes healing.
  • Aloe Vera – Hydrates and rejuvenates skin.
  • Rosehip – Rich in vitamin C and helps with skin regeneration.

Apply these herbs topically or take them in supplement form.
