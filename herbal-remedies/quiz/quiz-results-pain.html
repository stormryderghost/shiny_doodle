Herbal Remedies for Pain and Inflammation

For relief from pain and inflammation, consider the following herbs:

  • Turmeric – Contains curcumin, a powerful anti-inflammatory.
  • Willow Bark – Acts as a natural pain reliever.
  • Devil's Claw – Reduces inflammation and alleviates pain.

These herbs are effective as supplements or in tincture form.
