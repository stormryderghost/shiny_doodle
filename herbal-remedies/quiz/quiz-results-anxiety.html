Herbal Remedies for Anxiety and Stress

If you're dealing with anxiety and stress, try the following herbs:

  • Chamomile – Promotes relaxation and reduces stress.
  • Valerian – Helps calm nerves and improve sleep.
  • Lavender – Reduces anxiety and promotes relaxation.

Use these herbs as tea, essential oils, or in bath soaks.
