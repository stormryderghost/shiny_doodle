Herbal Remedies for Digestive Issues

If you're dealing with digestive problems, the following herbs can be beneficial:

  • Peppermint – Calms the digestive tract and relieves bloating.
  • Ginger – Eases nausea and supports digestion.
  • Fennel – Reduces gas and bloating.

For best results, try these herbs in tea or tincture form.
