Herbal Remedies for Immune Support

To boost your immune system, consider the following herbs:

  • Echinacea – Strengthens the immune response and fights infections.
  • Turmeric – Reduces inflammation and boosts immunity.
  • Elderberry – Rich in antioxidants and supports immune health.

These herbs can be taken as tea, supplements, or syrups.
