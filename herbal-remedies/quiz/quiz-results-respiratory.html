Herbal Remedies for Respiratory Issues

If you're dealing with respiratory problems, the following herbs can provide relief:

  • Mullein – Soothes the respiratory tract and reduces inflammation.
  • Licorice Root – Relieves sore throats and acts as an expectorant.
  • Eucalyptus – Helps clear sinuses and ease breathing.

Use these herbs in tea, lozenges, or steam inhalations.
