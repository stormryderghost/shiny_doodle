How Ginger Helps with Digestive Health

  • Reduces Nausea: Ginger has been widely researched for its anti-nausea properties, especially in pregnant women and individuals undergoing chemotherapy. It can be consumed in tea form or as a supplement. (Healthline)
  • Improves Digestion: Ginger increases the digestive enzymes in your stomach, helping food pass more easily through the digestive tract.
  • Relieves Bloating and Gas: Ginger is often used to reduce bloating and flatulence by relaxing the muscles in your intestines, which can help ease discomfort after eating.
