                <p>Herbal tinctures are concentrated liquid extracts made from herbs. They are easy to make at home and can be used for various health benefits.</p>
                
                <h2>How to Make Herbal Tinctures</h2>
                <ul>
                    <li>Select your herbs (e.g., echinacea, chamomile, or valerian).</li>
                    <li>Place the herbs in a jar and cover them with alcohol (vodka or brandy).</li>
                    <li>Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 4-6 weeks, shaking occasionally.</li>
                    <li>Strain the mixture and store the tincture in a dropper bottle.</li>
                </ul>
