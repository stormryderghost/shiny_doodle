                <h2>Notable Herbalists</h2>
                <p><strong>Hildegard of Bingen</strong>: A 12th-century abbess who wrote extensively about herbal medicine in her work "Physica," offering detailed descriptions of various plants and their uses.</p>
                
                <p><strong>Nicholas Culpeper</strong>: An English herbalist from the 17th century known for his work "The English Physician," which made herbal medicine accessible to the general public by translating complex medical knowledge into practical advice.</p>
                
                <p><strong>John Gerard</strong>: An English botanist and herbalist who authored "The Herball, or Generall Historie of Plantes," a comprehensive guide to plants and their uses, published in 1597.</p>
